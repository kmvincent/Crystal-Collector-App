You are given a randomized target number at the start of the game. There are four buttons above. By clicking on a button you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to the targeted number, you lose if your total score goes above the random number. The value of each crystal is hidden from you. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
